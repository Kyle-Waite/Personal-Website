This is my Resources Page below are some of the websites and forums I frequent. They can to help understanding coding concept and tackle problems. These are some powerful website so feel free to check them out.

Team Treehouse

Team Treehouse is one of the most brilliant dedicated online developer academy's. With user, friendly videos and exercise it's a go to resource.

HackerRank

HackerRank provides a feel for real life industry problems and solutions with vibrant a community of dedicated to helping you solve and understand them.

Stackoverflow

Have a piece of code thats got you stumped? Then refer to stackoverflow here developers gather to share solutions and tackle problems.

Mozilla Developer Network

Mozilla Developer Network (MDN) is the developer dictionary. When faced with confusing concepts MDN will act as your marauders map to success.

Codeacademy

Want some hands-on training? Then Codeacademy is the place for you with thousands of exercises to honing your coding skills.

DevU

Bob Taber opens the door with DevU the site is filled with interactive videos and exercises sure to keep you engaged.
